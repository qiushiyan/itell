<Keyterm label="Head Start program">
  a program for early childhood education directed at families with limited
  educational and financial resources.
</Keyterm>

<Keyterm label="Ricardian equivalence">
  the theory that rational private households might shift their saving to offset
  government saving or borrowing
</Keyterm>

<Keyterm label="twin deficits">
  deficits that occur when a country is running both a trade and a budget
  deficit
</Keyterm>
