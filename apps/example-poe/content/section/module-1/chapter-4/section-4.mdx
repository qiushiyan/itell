<Keyterm label="financial services markets ">
  are markets for saving or borrowing
</Keyterm>

<Keyterm label="interest rate">
  the “price” of borrowing in the financial market; a rate of return on an
  investment
</Keyterm>

<Keyterm label="loanable funds">
  include all forms of credit, such as loans, bonds, or savings deposits
</Keyterm>

<Keyterm label="labor markets">are markets for employees or jobs</Keyterm>

<Keyterm label="minimum wage ">
  a price floor that makes it illegal for an employer to pay employees less than
  a certain hourly rate
</Keyterm>

<Keyterm label="rate of return">
  is a price variable in the financial markets (e.g. interest rate)
</Keyterm>

<Keyterm label="usury laws">
  laws that impose an upper limit on the interest rate that lenders can charge
</Keyterm>
